Challenge 4: Medical procurement 

https://youtu.be/wecfweLwb0U 

Simply buying medical equipment can be a challenging and work-intensive task for NHS trusts, with complex processes of finding suppliers, selecting appropriate items and getting the purchase approved. The aim of this project would be to make NHS procurement more like e-commerce by creating an “amazon for the NHS” that aggregates equipment vendors and includes features like reviews from expert staff.
